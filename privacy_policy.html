Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the Second Brain application (the "Service") and tells you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Second Brain is designed as an offline-first, local-only application. All your data, including but not limited to:

  • Notes
  • Images
  • Voice Notes
  • Bookmarks
  • Documents
  • Collections

is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, sync, or store your personal data on external servers or cloud databases.

2. Data Privacy and Security

Your privacy is our primary concern. Since the application operates entirely offline on your device, you have complete and absolute control over your data. We do not have access to any of the content you create or store within the app.

The app includes an optional PIN lock feature to help you secure your data locally from unauthorized physical access to your device.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or data collection tools that transmit your personal content off your device.

4. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
